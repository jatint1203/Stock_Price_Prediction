The Idea Behind This Project

We are a passionate team of students as investor. Earlier, we always faced problems while doing equity analysis in our personal capacity. Most Free stock screeners available either provided insufficient data or were plagued with inaccuracy.
The paid tools on the other hand, were way too expensive for the common man. We aim to make investing simple & rewarding for the retail investor. Hence, we took matters in our own hand to build an enterprise grade research & analysis tool for the retail investor and came up with this project.
